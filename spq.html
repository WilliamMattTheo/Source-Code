Roasted Sweet Potato & Quinoa Salad

Description:

A nourishing, plant-based salad packed with roasted sweet potatoes, fluffy quinoa, crunchy veggies, and a zesty lemon-tahini dressing. It's perfect as a light lunch, meal prep, or a colorful side dish.

Servings: 4

Prep Time: 15 minutes
Cook Time: 25 minutes
Total Time: 40 minutes
Vegan | Gluten-Free | High Fiber

Ingredients:

For the Salad:

  • 2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup quinoa (uncooked)
  • 2 cups water or vegetable broth
  • 1 cup canned chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1/2 small red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cucumber, diced
  • 2 cups baby spinach or mixed greens
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley (optional)

For the Dressing:

  • 3 tbsp tahini
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice (freshly squeezed)
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup or honey
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic, finely minced
  • 2/3 tbsp water (to thin, as needed)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Roast Sweet Potatoes: Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F). Toss cubed sweet potatoes with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Spread on a baking sheet and roast for 25 minutes, flipping halfway, until tender and caramelized.
  2. Cook Quinoa: Rinse quinoa under cold water. In a medium pot, combine quinoa and water/broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and cover. Simmer for 15 minutes or until water is absorbed. Remove from heat and fluff with a fork.
  3. Prepare Dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together tahini, lemon juice, maple syrup, olive oil, garlic, and enough water to reach a pourable consistency. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Assemble Salad: In a large bowl, combine cooked quinoa, roasted sweet potatoes, chickpeas, red onion, tomatoes, cucumber, and greens. Toss gently.
  5. Add Dressing: Drizzle dressing over the salad and toss to coat evenly. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.
  6. Serve: Serve chilled or at room temperature. Garnish with fresh parsley, if desired.
