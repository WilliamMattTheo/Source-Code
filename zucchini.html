Zucchini Noodle Stir Fry

Description:

A light, gluten-free, low-carb alternative to traditional stir fry, packed with vegetables and full of flavor. Perfect for a quick lunch or dinner!

Servings: 4

Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 10 minutes
Total Time: 20 minutes
Vegan | Gluten-Free | Low Carb | High Fiber

Ingredients:

For the Stir Fry:

  • 2 medium zucchinis, spiralized into noodles
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil or olive oil
  • 1 cup sliced bell peppers (red, yellow, or green)
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/2 cup snap peas
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp fresh ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp chopped scallions (optional)
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds (optional, for garnish)

For the Sauce:

  • 2 tbsp low-sodium soy sauce or tamari (for gluten-free)
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp maple syrup or honey
  • 1 tsp toasted sesame oil
  • 1/2 tsp sriracha or chili flakes (optional for spice)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the Sauce: In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, rice vinegar, maple syrup, sesame oil, and sriracha. Set aside.
  2. Sauté the Veggies: In a large pan or wok, heat sesame oil over medium-high heat. Add bell peppers, carrots, and snap peas. Stir-fry for 2–3 minutes until slightly tender but still crisp.
  3. Add Aromatics: Add garlic and ginger, and sauté for another minute until fragrant.
  4. Add Zucchini Noodles: Toss in the zucchini noodles and stir-fry for 2–3 minutes. Do not overcook—zoodles should be tender-crisp and not soggy.
  5. Combine with Sauce: Pour the sauce over the stir fry. Toss everything together for 1–2 minutes until well coated.
  6. Serve: Remove from heat, sprinkle with sesame seeds and chopped scallions if desired. Serve immediately!
